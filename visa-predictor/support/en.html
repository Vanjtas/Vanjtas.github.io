永住メーター (Eiju Meter)

Japanese Permanent Residency Application Progress Calculator

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this app do?

Eiju Meter is a tool that helps foreigners in Japan calculate their progress toward applying for permanent residency. By entering your residence status, time in Japan, and other information, you can estimate your readiness for a permanent residency application.

How do I use Eiju Meter?

Simply enter information such as your residence status type, time spent in Japan, Japanese language proficiency level, and other relevant details. The app will automatically calculate your permanent residency application readiness and provide relevant recommendations.

Are the calculation results accurate?

This app is designed based on publicly available permanent residency application guidelines, but it's for reference only and cannot replace professional legal advice. The final review results for permanent residency applications are determined by the Immigration Bureau of Japan.

The app won't start or crashes

Please try the following methods:

  • Ensure your device's system version meets the requirements
  • Clear the app cache
  • Reinstall the app
  • If the problem persists, please contact us using the email address below and provide your device model and system version

Please check for updates in the app store or visit our official website to download the latest version.
