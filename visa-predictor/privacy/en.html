Introduction

Thank you for choosing to use the Eiju Meter application. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. We value your privacy rights and are committed to protecting the security of your data.

Information Collection and Use

The Eiju Meter application collects the following information to provide services:

  • Device identifier (deviceId): Used to identify your device
  • Application-related information: Including permanent residency application date, current visa type, and application status updates (if the status is approved or denied, it will also include the date when the result was obtained)

We store this information in our database to provide you with better service and experience. We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or other information that could directly identify you.

Anonymous Usage Data

To improve the quality of our application and user experience, we may collect the following anonymous usage data:

  • Application performance data, such as crash reports
  • Feature usage frequency statistics
  • Device information (such as device model, operating system version)

This data does not contain personally identifiable information and is used only for application improvement.

Data Storage

Eiju Meter stores the collected deviceId and application-related information in our secure database to provide you with application status tracking and other features. Apart from this necessary information, we do not collect, store, or transmit any other personal data. Your privacy and security are important to us.

Third-Party Services

Our application may use the following third-party services:

  • App store distribution platforms (such as App Store, Google Play)
  • Crash analysis tools
  • Advertising services (if applicable)

These third-party services may have their own privacy policies, and we recommend that you review the privacy terms of these services.

Target Audience

The Eiju Meter application is primarily designed to help foreign residents in Japan calculate and track their permanent residency application progress. Based on the content and purpose of the application, it is primarily targeted at adult users, especially foreign residents who need to apply for permanent residency in Japan. The application itself does not contain any content directed at children and does not allow uploading any custom information.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page and notified within the application. We recommend that you periodically review this policy to stay informed of the latest changes.
